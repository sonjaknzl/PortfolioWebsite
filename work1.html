BOOKLET

This design work was part of a university project and involved the design of an icon set, a datavisualisation as well as a screendesign. The projects were summarised in a booklet presenting and explaining each project individually.

icon set design

Two icon sets were to be designed on the topic of sustainability using Adobe Illustrator.

These are the two developed icon sets covering the topics climate protection, biodiversity, politics, mindfulness, ecosystem, resources and renewable energies. The intention for both designs was a minimal and simple style with clear metaphors.

datavisualisation

A datavisulatisation in the context of sustainability was to be created. The topic of cryptocurrency and its impact on the environment were explored and displayed.

screendesign

For the screendesign an old timetable of the university was supposed to be redesigned. After exploring some key factors of timetables within the users, some first drafts were created.

A first high fidelity prototype was implemented. After various iterations and evaluating feedback from some test users the final design is displayed below.
